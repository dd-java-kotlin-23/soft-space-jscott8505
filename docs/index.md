## Summary

[//]: # Parents and caregivers with Autistic children often have to navigate complex daily routines,
such as unique support challenges, trying to find places that aren't going to overstimulate the
child, specific routines, etc. This app can help the parents or caregivers a chance to vent, or ask
for advice and connect to people who can understand and help with either giving suggestions,
recommending places or even making new friend groups who get it and help parents not feel alone. As
an autism parent i would love to connect to others who can understand what goes on a daily basis,
and not be judge by people who don't understand what people go through with special need kids.

## Intended users and user stories

[//]: Parents/caregivers
Siblings of special needs
Support groups
Special needs adults
-As a parent of an autistic 7 year old, I want to be able to find evens with sensory times and
events so my daughter doesn't get so overwhelmed and is still able to go out and enjoy playing.

-As an autistic adult I would love to tell people how I can function or regulate myself in
overwhelming situations. I would also love to find evens for people like me, it would make such a
difference to be able to talk to others who may understand or even who needs advice on what they can
do for their children.
